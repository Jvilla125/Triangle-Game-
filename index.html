Welcome to the Triangle game

How to Play

The objective of the game is to ump the pegs and remove them from the game.

Rules

  1. You start by removing any random peg from the board.
  2. Every jump must be over a neighboring peg.
  3. There needs to be an empty space for the peg to land on. (Can be horizontally or diagonally)
  4. After you jump over a peg, it is removed from the game.
